Sofia is a third-year student in the Lyu lab applying ultra-large library docking to identify novel chemical probes for understudied GPCRs. Her work integrates computational screening of billion-compound libraries with rapid medicinal chemistry optimization and pharmacological characterization. She received her BS in Biochemistry and Mathematics from the University of Texas at Austin.

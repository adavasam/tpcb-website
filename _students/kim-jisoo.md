Jisoo is a second-year student in the Tan lab developing enantioselective synthetic routes to complex terpenoid natural products with anticancer activity. Her thesis focuses on establishing structure-activity relationships within a class of marine-derived compounds that inhibit a clinically relevant oncology target. She received her BS in Chemistry from Seoul National University.

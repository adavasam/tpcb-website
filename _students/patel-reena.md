Reena is a second-year student in the David lab developing semisynthetic approaches to study histone modification crosstalk in cancer. She synthesizes chemically modified nucleosomes to dissect the molecular mechanisms by which specific histone marks coordinate gene silencing. Reena received her BA in Chemistry from Columbia University.

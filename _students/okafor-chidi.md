Chidi is a fourth-year student in the Brady lab investigating natural product biosynthetic gene clusters from the gut microbiome of diverse human populations. He uses a combination of heterologous expression, metabolomics, and structural characterization to identify novel bioactive compounds with antibacterial properties. He received his BS in Chemistry from Howard University.

Minho is a second-year student in the Lyu lab using virtual screening and structure-based drug design to identify selective modulators of voltage-gated ion channels implicated in chronic pain. He combines large-scale docking with machine learning models trained on electrophysiology data. He received his BS in Chemistry from KAIST.

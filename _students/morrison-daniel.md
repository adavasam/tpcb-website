Daniel is a fifth-year student finishing his dissertation on predicting kinase inhibitor selectivity using alchemical free energy calculations. He has developed a benchmark dataset for evaluating free energy methods against experimental selectivity data across a panel of 50 kinases. He is co-advised through the Open Force Field Consortium. He received his BS in Biophysics from Johns Hopkins University.

Hana is a fourth-year student in the David lab studying the role of non-canonical histone variants in transcriptional regulation in cancer. She uses semisynthetic chemistry, proximity labeling, and genomic approaches to understand how specific histone modifications are read and written in tumor cells. She received her BS in Biochemistry and Cell Biology from Rice University.

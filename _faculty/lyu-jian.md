The Lyu lab develops and applies computational and experimental approaches to discover novel drug candidates from ultra-large chemical libraries containing billions of compounds. The lab pioneered docking of billion-compound libraries against G protein-coupled receptors and other therapeutic targets, revealing new chemotypes with unprecedented selectivity profiles. Current research spans target identification, lead optimization, and the integration of machine learning into the drug discovery pipeline.

The Chodera lab develops rigorous statistical mechanical methods for understanding how small molecules bind to proteins, with the goal of enabling structure-based drug discovery. The lab is a leader in open-source computational chemistry software, including the OpenMM simulation toolkit and the Open Force Field Initiative. Current projects focus on machine learning force fields, alchemical free energy methods, and automated drug discovery pipelines.

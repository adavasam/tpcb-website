The Brady lab is a leader in the discovery of new natural products from environmental DNA (eDNA) and the human microbiome. The lab develops metagenomic approaches to mine the biosynthetic potential of uncultured microorganisms, recovering entire biosynthetic gene clusters and expressing them in heterologous hosts to produce novel bioactive compounds. Current projects explore the chemical ecology of the human microbiome and develop new tools for genome-guided natural product discovery.

title: "Brady lab awarded $3.2M NIH grant for microbiome natural product discovery"
tags: [faculty, awards]

The Brady lab at The Rockefeller University has received a five-year, $3.2 million R01 grant from the National Institutes of Health to continue their pioneering work on natural product discovery from the human microbiome. The project will develop new computational and experimental tools to identify and characterize the thousands of biosynthetic gene clusters encoded in the microbiomes of diverse human populations.

This grant will support three TPCB graduate students and two postdoctoral fellows in the Brady lab, continuing the program's tradition of training chemical biologists at the frontier of metagenomics and natural product chemistry.

title: "Lyu lab publishes landmark study on billion-compound docking in Nature Chemical Biology"
tags: [publications, faculty]
---

A new paper from the Lyu lab at Weill Cornell Medicine, published in *Nature Chemical Biology*, reports the successful docking and experimental validation of over two billion compounds against a panel of disease-relevant protein targets. The study identifies several structurally novel lead compounds for targets previously considered undruggable.

The work demonstrates that ultra-large library docking, when combined with rapid parallel synthesis and cell-based screening, can dramatically accelerate the earliest stages of drug discovery. Three of the identified leads are now advancing toward preclinical development in collaboration with industry partners.

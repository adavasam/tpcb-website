TPCB student Gabriella Chua (Tan Lab, MSK) has been named a recipient of the 2026 Harold Weintraub Graduate Student Award from the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center. The award recognizes exceptional achievement and outstanding promise in the field of basic science by graduate students.

Gabriella was recognized for her work developing novel synthetic routes to natural product scaffolds with potent anticancer activity. Her research has uncovered new mechanisms by which small molecules can selectively kill cancer cells while sparing normal tissue. The award includes an invitation to present her work at the annual Weintraub Symposium in Seattle.

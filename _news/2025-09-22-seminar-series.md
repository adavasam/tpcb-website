The TPCB Chemical Biology Seminar Series has announced its Fall 2025 lineup, featuring eight distinguished speakers from academia and industry. Seminars are held on alternating Thursdays at Weill Cornell Medicine and The Rockefeller University, and are open to all members of the tri-institutional community.

Highlights include lectures by leaders in RNA chemical biology, covalent drug discovery, and structural enzymology. The series continues to be one of the program's most valued educational offerings, providing students direct access to the world's leading chemical biologists and fostering connections between the three campuses on the Upper East Side.

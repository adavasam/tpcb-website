tags: [students]

The Tri-Institutional PhD Program in Chemical Biology is pleased to announce the incoming cohort for the 2026 academic year. Eight exceptional students from across the United States and internationally have joined the program, bringing expertise in chemistry, biochemistry, biology, and computational science.

The new cohort will spend their first year completing laboratory rotations across all three institutions before selecting a thesis laboratory. The program welcomed the incoming class at its annual orientation retreat at the Rockefeller University Field Research Center on Long Island.
